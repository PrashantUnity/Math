# About Math Explorer

**Math Explorer** is an educational platform dedicated exclusively to **Mathematics** for students from **Class 6 and above**.

Our goal is to make math simple, intuitive, and fun through step-by-step guides, visual geometric proofs, real-world examples, and interactive problem-solving techniques.

---

## 📚 What You Will Learn Here

- 🔢 **Number Systems & Arithmetic**: Negative numbers, Integers, Fractions, Decimals, Ratio & Proportion, and Percentages.
- 📐 **Geometry & Shapes**: Lines, Angles, Triangles, Quadrilaterals, Circles, Perimeter, and Area formulas.
- 🔣 **Algebraic Thinking**: Variables, Expressions, Linear Equations, Polynomials, and Functions.
- 📊 **Data Handling & Probability**: Graphs, Statistics, Mean, Median, Mode, and Probability basics.
- 💡 **Speed Math & Tricks**: Mental math short-cuts, Vedic math techniques, and Olympiad problem-solving tricks.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

- **Class 6 – Class 8**: Building strong foundational concepts in Integers, Fractions, Algebra, and Geometry.
- **Class 9 – Class 10**: Mastering Quadratic Equations, Coordinate Geometry, Trigonometry, and Mensuration.
- **Class 11 – Class 12 & Beyond**: Calculus, Vectors, Probability, Matrices, and Competitive Exam Foundations.
